Behind the Scenes: Documenting Reality

This project uses Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF), Structure-from-Motion (SfM) and 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) photogrammetry to recreate museum spaces in exquisite detail.

  • DSLR photo scans (~500–1500 photos per exhibit)
  • COLMAP
  • NerfStudio
  • Three.js WebGL viewer
  • Gaussian Splatting 3D
